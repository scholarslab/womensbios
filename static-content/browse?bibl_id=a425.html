East Aurora, NY: Roycrofter's, 1898; 1901; 1908. New York: Wise; E. Aurora: Roycrofters, 1911; 1916; 1923. Little Journeys to the Homes of the Great, Vol. 2: Famous Women. New York: Wise, 1923-28. Cleveland: World, 1928.
